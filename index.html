About the Community

Description

LOGIC SYNC is a large hybrid Technical Community being constructed by the students of TIIPS, knowledge park 2 , greater noida. The first round would be conducted through an online meeting, where participants would have to present their projects to their appointed mentors.
This would then be followed by the main & final judgement round, which would be conducted offline in TIIPS ,knowledge park 2 ,greater noida.

For any queries, feel free to contact us anytime.
